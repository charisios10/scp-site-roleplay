SCP: Site Roleplay

This is a small page for new players of the SCP: Site Roleplay to learn more things about the game.

1.2 Teams

O5 Command

The O5 Command team is one of the most powerfull people in the game beacuase they are the some what called "CEOs" of the site. They have the tools to detain wich other people cant, also the o5 command have the autharization to acess control room and vote and get the keys for nuking the facility if needed.

Site Director (SiD)

The SiD is a "budget" version as they call it of an o5. This role has everything from o5 but they only can cuff foundation personel and cds and not detain and they can only vote for nuke and not take the keys.

Intelligence Agency (IA)

The IA team is a like a investigetor about how breaches are made to find a traitor or cuff abusers. Most of the times this role is used by abusers to cuff abuse or even make commands like sid+ can do.

Rapid Response Team (RRT)

The rrt team is like a better version of the mtf team and they are suposed to be the first to help and response to breaches. They also have the ability to cuff cds that usally only O5, SiD, IA and SD can do.

Moblie Task Force (MTF)

The mtf is a group of teams that help secure, contain and protect the foundation personel and cds. The MTF is grouped in 4 groups, the Alpha-1, Beta-7, Epsilon-11 and Nu-7.

Alpha-1 ("Red Right Hand")

The alpha-1 group is supposed to protect the o5 and sid council, also they are the highest group in clearance beacuase in case you want to join the team you will need to have L-3+ Clearance.

Beta-7 ("Maz Hatters")

The Beta-7 is specialized and equiped with gas masks to recontain or help with biological breaches. This teams needs no clearance to have to be part of it.

Epsilon-11 ("Nine-Tailed Fox")

The Epsilon-11 is supposed to provide a quick response to any threats that are currently on the facility but most of the time they recontain and chill in cdc. This team also doesnt need any clearance to join.

Nu-7 ("Hammer Down")

The Nu-7 is another group that is specialized and equiped with special and heavy equipement to response to overwelming scenarios that need heavy firepower as like terminating any CI or Class-d Riot, But most of the time they are in cdc protecting Foundation Personel.

Security Department (SD)

The SD team is specialized in protecting and terminating any threads inside of cdc, but most of the times new players choose this team and abuse like RK(Random Killing) or RS (Random Shooting) cds or as a joke that they say "They are shooting at everything that moves".
